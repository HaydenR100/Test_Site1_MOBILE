About Us

Low Cost A/C Repair

Moody A/C focuses on the heating and cooling needs of Mid County and Beaumont area. We have been serving this community for decades, and feel that our local customers are the cornerstone of our business. Our A+ rating and around the clock telephone contact are just one of the aspects locals trust about our service. In addition, all repairs are backed by a per-part manufacturer warranty and a 30-day warranty on our labor as well.
  1. Professional - Our focus has always been on providing dependable, professional service at a reasonable price. As a result, when our team shows up to work, we are committed to punctuality and discretion, working unnoticed to provide you with a stellar level of care that will not disrupt your home or business.
  2. Knowledgeable - With years of service by highly-trained professionals and the most up to date technology, there aren’t a whole lot of issues we haven’t seen before or challenges we haven’t tackled in this business.
  3. Focused on Efficiency - At Moody A/C we know heating and cooling costs can be high, and they just seem to keep rising. That is why we are fully committed to reducing the energy required to heat or cool your area. This not only has a beneficially impact on your wallet, but also reduces your impact on the environment as well. We strive to leave buildings and dwellings running at lower cost with less environmental impact as well.
Moody Air Conditioning Corp. was formed in late 2009 in Nederland, TX after its founder John Moody. Our mission is to deliver quality heating and cooling instillation and repair at a reasonable price. Because of the value and quality of their products, we rely on Carrier as our main supplier of systems and parts.
